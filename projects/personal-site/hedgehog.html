Vali the Hedgehog

This is Vali the hedgehog. He is about two and a half months old. He has been with me for about half that long. He is about the size of a baseball when he is curled up in a ball. He enjoys climing on top of his house and exploring my apartment.

Fun facts about Hedgehogs

  • They are nocturnal.
  • They eat a tablespoon of cat food a day.
  • They can curl up into a ball of spikes.
  • The muscles on their back allow them to individualy control their spines
  • They are not related to the porcupine, but intstead are closly related to the vole
